Chicken Murphy

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Salt & Pepper
  • White Wine
  • Carrots, Potatoes, Bell Pepper
  • Chicken Broth

Cooking Directions

  1. Cut chicken into equal quarters
  2. Salt and pepper to taste in bowl with white wine
  3. Cut carrots, potatoes, and bell pepper into equal parts
  4. Add all ingredients with chicken broth into cooker
  5. Cook til its done
