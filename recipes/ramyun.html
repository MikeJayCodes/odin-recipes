Ramyun Noodles

Ingredients

  • Package of Shin Ramyun Nooodle
  • Egg

Directions

  1. Fill pot with water up to the first joint on your index finger
  2. Once water reaches a boil place in noodles and broke boy veggies
  3. Allow noodle to break apart and add seasoning
  4. When noodles are 2mins from being done crack egg and do not touch
  5. Remove from heat and serve
